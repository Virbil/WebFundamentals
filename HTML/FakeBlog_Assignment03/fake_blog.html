My father-in-law is building us a home. I thought this process would be fun. Parts of it have been...

There are many reasons why you should consider building a custom home

  • Allows you to be creative with the design and decor
  • Get exactly what you want
  • The home becomes your vision and passion

However, there are also some things you should know with custom homes

  • You make EVERY decision. Like how many outlets you have and where do they go
  • Everything takes longer than you think
  • The expense can get away from your budget real quick
